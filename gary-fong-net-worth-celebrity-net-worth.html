What is Gary Fong's Net Worth?

Gary Fong is an American entrepreneur who has a net worth of $20 million. Gary Fong was born in Seattle, Washington, and was studying to be a pharmacologist when he learned about wedding photography. He began working with famed wedding photographer, Rocky Gunn, but Mr. Gunn passed away suddenly. Gary Fong launched his own photography business, and went on to revolutionize how weddings were shot, including creating the "Storybook Concept", or the process of shooting wedding albums sequentially, like a storyboard.

He became immensely famous, and is widely considered one of the top ten wedding photographers in the world. Some of his past clients include Sylvester Stallone, Paul McCartney, Todd Rundgren, and Pia Zadora, to name a few. In 2002, he began focusing on other ventures, and has since designed popular lighting equipment, launched a personal print lab called Pictage that sold for $29 million, and written a popular memoir.
