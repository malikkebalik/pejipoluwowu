What is Aditya Chopra's Net Worth?

Aditya Chopra is an Indian producer, director, screenwriter, and distributor who has a net worth of $30 million dollars. Aditya Chopra was born in Mumbai, Meharashtra, India, and graduated from Hassaram Rijhumal College of Commerce and Economics. The son of famed filmmaker, Yash Chopra, Aditya Chopra began assisting his father on film projects when he was in his teens. When he was 23, he branched out on his own, and wrote and directed the independent feature, "Dilwale Dulhaniya Le Jayenge". The film became one of India's most successful films ever and won multiple awards.

Working with his late father and is brothers, the family launched their own production company, Yash Raj Films. As a writer, director, and producer under the Yash Raj banner, Aditya Chopra has created some of India's most popular films. YRF now has home entertainment, music, and television divisions, as well. YRF, which is currently under Aditya Chopra's direction, is now recognized as the most successful film studio in India.
