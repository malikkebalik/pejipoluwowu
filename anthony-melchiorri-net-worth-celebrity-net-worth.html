What is Anthony Melchiorri's Net Worth?

Anthony Melchiorri is an American television personality and hospitality expert who has a net worth of $2 million. Anthony Melchiorri was born in May 1965 and raised in Brooklyn, New York. He is best known for being the creator, co-executive producer, and Host of the Travel Channel television series Hotel Impossible. Anthony joined the United States Air Force out of high school. While finishing his college degree he worked as a night auditor at an Embassy Suites. He later became a night manager at the Plaza Hotel on Fifth Avenue in New York City. He then worked up to Director of Front Office Operations. Melchiorri became general manager of the Lucerne Hotel which was selected as the New York Times Travel Guide's Best Service Hotel. Anthony oversaw a $15 million renovation of the Algonquin Hotel. He later became senior vice president of the first Nickelodeon Hotel and Resort and was the first vice president of Tishman Hotels and senior vice president of New York Hotel Management Company. He founded his own hotel management and consultancy Argeo Hospitality. As host of Hotel Impossible he fixes major problems with hotels. The show has been on the air since 2012 and he is also involved with Hotel Impossible: Undercover starting in 2014.
