Sandra Fenwick biography

Sandra L. Fenwick serves as Director of Acusphere Inc., since September 2004. Ms. Fenwick is President and Chief Operating Officer of the Children's Hospital Boston in Boston, Massachusetts. Prior to joining Children's Hospital in 1999, Ms. Fenwick was Senior Vice President of System Development for CareGroup, Inc. Before its merger with CareGroup, Ms. Fenwick served at Beth Israel Hospital for 20 years in various areas of increasing responsibility including Vice President and Deputy Director, Vice President of Clinical Services Planning and Development and Vice President of Network Development for Beth Israel Healthcare.
