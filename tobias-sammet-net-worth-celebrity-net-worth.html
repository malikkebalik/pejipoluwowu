What is Tobias Sammet's Net Worth?

Tobias Sammet is a German musician and radio host who has a net worth of $3 million. Tobias Sammet was born in Fulda, Germany in November 1977. He is the vocalist and songwriter for the heavy metal band Edguy and for the rock opera project Avantasia. Sammet was named best songwriter in 2011 by Burrn Magazine Japan. All together he has sold more than three millions albums worldwide. Edguy formed in 1992 and released their debut studio album Kingdom of Madness in 1997.

They have had Top 10 albums in Sweden or Germany with their albums Hellfire Club in 2004, Rocket Ride in 2006, Age of the Joker in 2011, and Space Police: Defenders of the Crown in 2014.
