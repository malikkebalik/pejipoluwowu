Professional Career: Movies and TV Shows

Before Shin Min Ah flourished as an actress, she started her career as a model. In 1998, she began modeling for a teen magazine named KiKi. Soon after that, Min Ah became the most in-demand model.

With her charm and looks, and of course, hard work, this diva became the highest-paid commercial endorser in South Korea.

Likewise, she worked with many top brands for campaigns, as well as commercials.

In 2009, Min Ah flew to Milan, Italy, to shoot for Elle Korea with Calvin Klein model Jamie Dornan.

Also, in 2011, she was selected as the first Asian model for the DIY Project ad campaign of the American brand Rag & Bone. 

After dominating the modeling industry, Min Ah moved toward acting. She first appeared in several music videos for K-pop boyband g.o.d. And finally, in 2001, Min Ah made her acting debut in the melodrama, Beautiful Days.

Furthermore, Min Ah was hit among the show audience as well. Soon after, the offer came flooding as she was seen in numerous movies and TV Shows.

Some of Min Ah’s remarkable works can be seen in productions like Volcano High (2010, Madeleine (2003), Sad Movie (2005), Go-Go 70s (2008), The Naked Kitchen (2009), Gyeongju (2014), My Love, My Bride (2014), Oh My Venus (2015-2016), Tomorrow, With You (2017), Chief of Staff (2019), and many more.

Aside from acting, Min Ah has also contributed her vocals to several singles and soundtracks.

Awards and Accolades

Shin Min Ah is not only loved by her fans but praised for her professionalism and acting as well. And it seems critics also agree with them. In 2003, Min Ah got her first SBS Drama Award in the New Star category for Punch.

Likewise, Min Ah also bagged the 2006’s Diro Timeless Beauty Award. However, it was the movie Go Go 70s, that brought her critical acclaim. 

For this drama alone, she won various awards, including the 4th University Film Festival of Korea, the 6th Max Movie Awards, and the 17th Chunsa Film Art Awards. 

Moreover, for the drama, My Girlfriend is a Nine-Tailed Fox, which was a huge hit both in Korea and the international market, Min Ah won almost all the awards, including Excellence Awards and Best Couple.
