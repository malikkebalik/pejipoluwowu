What is Hector Jimenez's Net Worth?

Hector Jimenez is a Mexican actor who has a net worth of $2 million. Hector Jimenez was born in Tijuana, Baja California, Mexico in December 1973. He is best known for starring as Steven/Esqueleto in the movie Nacho Libre in 2006. Jimenez has also starred in the films Epic Movie in 2007, Wild Hogs in 2007, Killer Pad in 2007, Sin Nombre in 2009, Gentlemen Broncos in 2009, Sharktopus in 2010, Cellmates in 2011, Casa de Mi Padre in 2012, and El Santos vs.

la Tetona Mendeoza in 2012. Jimenez was nominated for an MTV Movie Award and two Teen Choice Awards for Nacho Libre. He has been referred to as "The Mexican Steve Buscemi".
