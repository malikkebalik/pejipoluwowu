Bobbi Kristina Brown Awakens From Coma, Off Life Support: Bobby Brown and Aunt Tina Brown Dreaming?

Publish date: 2024-08-10

Bobby Brown shocked the world when he told everyone that Bobbi Kristina Brown was awake during his concert in Texas, and now, Tina Brown has taken to her Facebook to ‘confirm’ the news. In fact, Tina Brown wrote a long Facebook post about Bobbi being awake, writing, “So many messages,sorry can’t answer them all right now but I will get to them later..just allow me this time with my family,,yes,she woke up and is no longer on life support!!!!!:):)God is good!!thanks for your prayers,,still a process,but she is going to be ok:):):):).”

So right now, more than one person is claiming that Bobbi Kristina Brown is off life support and has woken up, even though we haven’t seen any evidence supporting that. Honestly, it’s very VERY possible that Bobby Brown and Tina Brown are lying, and it could be for a multitude of reasons. One reason is that whenever Bobby leaves Bobbi Kristina Brown, he might be afraid that Pat Houston and Cissy Houston are going to try to remove Bobbi off life support.

Another possible reason is that Bobby and Tina might be attempting to ward off Cissy Houston and Pat Houston [and really, the rest of that greedy family] from attempting to collect the $20 million from Bobbi Kristina Brown’s inheritance. It’s almost like that story in Scandal where the characters attempt to lie about the president waking up from the coma, except this is real life and there is a LOT at stake – not to mention, if Bobbi really isn’t awake, everyone’s bound to find out eventually.
