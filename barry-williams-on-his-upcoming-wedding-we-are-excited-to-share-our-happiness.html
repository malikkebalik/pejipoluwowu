Publish date: 2024-07-28

Congratulations are in order for Barry Williams — he's getting ready to tie the knot!

In an exclusive new interview with Closer Weekly, the 62-year-old actor gushed about his upcoming wedding to his fiancée, Tina Mahina.

This will be Barry's third marriage.
