The current feud between motivational speaker Rendon Labador and actor-director Coco Martin is over Rendon Labador’s presence on Martin’s television show, “FPJ’s Batang Quiapo.” Here’s a rundown of what transpired between the two people.

What Happened Between Rendon Labador And Coco Martin? Controversy Explained

Rendon Labador, a social media figure, rushed to Facebook yesterday and today to protest that his new restaurant, Episode Bar + Kitchen, has had no sales so far — and that admirers of Actress Coco Martin may be to blame.

On March 25, Labador alleged that Martin’s followers did him in after his restaurant had no ticket sales. He claimed that his expenses totaled 1.2 million dollars. On March 26, Labador accused Martin’s followers of being impoverished since they couldn’t pay for his restaurant ticket.

Labador, a motivational speaker, has been hurling insults at Martin, the star of the TV series “Batang Quiapo,” whose location shooting in Quiapo has reportedly harmed the daily sales of Quiapo vendors.
