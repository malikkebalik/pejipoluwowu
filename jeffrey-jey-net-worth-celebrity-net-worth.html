What is Jeffrey Jey's Net Worth?

Jeffrey Jey is an Italian singer-songwriter who has a net worth of $5 million. Jeffrey Jey was born in Lentini, Italy in January 1970. He is the lead singer of the band Eiffel 65 and the former lead singer of the bands Bliss Team and Bloom 06. Eiffel 65 released their debut studio album Europop in 1999 and the album reached #4 in the United States and New Zealand. They released the albums Contact!

in 2001 and Eiffel 65 in 2003. Their best known single "Blue (Da Ba Dee)" reached #1 in eight countries and their follow up single "Move Your Body" was also very successful. Bliss Team was an Italian Eurodance group who were known for their hits including "Love Is Forever", "You Make Me Cry", and "People Have The Power". Bloom 06 was an Italian electronic dance group who released the albums Crash Test 01 and Crash Test 02. Jey plays bass guitar, electric guitar, keyboards, and drums as well as vocals.
