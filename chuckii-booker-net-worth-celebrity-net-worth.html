What is Chuckii Booker's Net Worth?

Chuckii Booker is an American singer, songwriter, musician, bandleader, and producer who has a net worth of $3 million. Chuckii Booker was born in Los Angeles, California in December 1966. Booker was signed by his godfather Barry White in 1984. He played keyboards with the band Tease for three years. Booker's debut studio album Chuckii was released on Atlantic Records in 1989. The album reached #18 on the US R&B charts. His second album Niice 'n Wild was released in 1992 and hit #13 on the US R&B charts. Booker had #1 hits on the US R&B charts with the singles "Turned Away" in 1989 and "Games" in 1992.

His other singles include (Don't U Know) I Love U", "Touch", and "I Should Have Loved You". He produced for several artists such as Troop, Barry White, Vanessa Williams, Janet Jackson, En Vogue, Diana Ross, Lionel Richie, Bette Midler, and Kool & the Gang. Booker served as music director and keyboardist for TLC on their CrazySexyCool tour. He also toured with new Edition, Faith Evans, Tina Turner, and Mary J. Blige. Booker co-owns the recording and mixing studio Aire L.A. Studios.
