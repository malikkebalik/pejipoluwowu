Scottie Morris Missing And Linked To Jewelry Store Robbery San Ramon

Publish date: 2024-07-28

Authorities in Indiana are urgently searching for Scottie Morris, a 14-year-old boy who has been reported missing since Thursday.

Scottie Morris, a 14-year-old boy from Eaton, Indiana, has gone missing since Thursday night, and Police believe he may be in extreme danger and require medical assistance.

The state Police have issued a statewide alert, and a massive search is underway to locate him. However, as the investigation progresses, some clues link Morris to a jewelry store robbery in San Ramon, California.

Scottie Morris Missing

According to local Police, Morris was last seen on Thursday at 8:30 pm and was reported missing the same night. Eaton is a small town located about 75 miles northeast of Indianapolis.

When Morris didn’t return home, his family and the local Police began searching for him. After a few hours of searching, they reported him missing, and Indiana State Police issued a silver alert.

The search for Morris has been extensive and has included hundreds of people, including local Police, Fire departments, school district leaders, classmates, and volunteers.

The search has been primarily focused on fields, forests, and rivers in the area, and various resources, such as infrared drones, bloodhound teams, and boats, have been deployed to find him. However, as of yet, Morris remains missing.

The community has rallied together to find Morris. Likewise, there has been an outpouring of support for the Morris family.

Volunteers have come from all over to help with the search, and local businesses have offered their support in any way they can. The community is hoping for the safe return of Morris and is doing everything it can to help.

Scottie Morris Linked to Jewelry Store Robbery San Ramon

While the search for Morris is ongoing, there is a new development that has caught the attention of the Police. Morris was linked to a robbery at a jewelry store in San Ramon, California, the same day he went missing.

The robbery occurred around 12:30 pm on Thursday. Likewise, the robbers made off with jewelry worth thousands of dollars.

The Police have released surveillance footage of the robbery, which shows two men entering the store, smashing the display cases with hammers, and grabbing the jewelry. The footage shows that the robbers were wearing hoodies and masks.

However, the Police identified one of the robbers as Morris based on his distinctive walk. The Police have not yet confirmed whether Morris was involved in the robbery, but the evidence is compelling.

The Police believe Morris may have traveled to California to participate in the robbery and are investigating this lead. The San Ramon Police Department has issued a statement urging anyone with information about Morris to come forward.

The Police have also released a statement assuring the public that they are doing everything possible to solve the robbery and find the suspects. The investigation into the robbery is ongoing, and the Police are pursuing all leads.

They are working with law enforcement agencies nationwide to track Morris and the other suspect. The Police have also contacted the public, asking anyone who may have seen Morris or have any information about the robbery to come forward.

The community is shocked by the news that Morris may have been involved in the robbery. Similarly, many are struggling to understand what could have led him to participate in such a crime.

The Morris family has released a statement expressing their shock and disbelief and asking for privacy as they work through this difficult time.
