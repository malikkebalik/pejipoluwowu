John Deere 6300 Utility Tractor is equipped with a John Deere CD4039 turbocharged diesel 4-cylinder liquid-cooled engine, which delivers 64 hp (47.7 kW) power at rated rpm 2300.

John Deere 6300 Utility Tractor featured a John Deere 3.9L 4-cyl diesel engine, 4×2 2WD or 4×4 MFWD 4WD, hydrostatic power steering, hydraulic wet disc brakes, 4,900 lbs Rear lift (at 24″/610mm) and 7497 to 9645 pounds weight.

John Deere, 6300 Utility tractor attachments include John Deere 620 Loader, John Deere 640NSL Loader, and John Deere 640SL Loader.
