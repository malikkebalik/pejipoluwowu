Cops Called to Jenelle Evans' Home for 'Family Offenses'

Publish date: 2024-08-25

Credit: MTV

This is certainly not the first time that cops have made it out to The Land…

New reports reveal that Jenelle Evans has called the cops on David Eason multiple times over a domestic dispute since their separation. She claimed that David was guilty of larceny and other “family offenses.”

The former Teen Mom 2 star filed for legal separation on February 23rd. According to court papers, she wants full custody of their daughter Ensley. The couple later revealed their split on social media and has been publicly battling it out since.
