What is Andra Day's Net Worth?

Andra Day is an American singer and songwriter who has a net worth of $4 million. Andra Day was born in Spokane, Washington in December 1984. Her musical styles include soul, R&B, blues, disco, and pop. Andra Day released her debut studio album Cheers to the Fall in 2015 which reached #3 on the US R&B chart, #6 on the US R&B/Hip-Hop chart, and #48 on the Billboard 200 chart. She also released the EP Merry Christmas from Andra Day in 2016.

Day's single "Rise Up" reached #1 on the US Bubbling Under Hot 100 Singles chart. It also reached #13 on the US R&B chart and in Scotland as well as #19 on the UK Download chart and #31 on the US R&B/Hip-Hop chart. Andra Day's single "Someday at Christmas" (featuring Stevie Wonder) reached #24 on the US R&B chart. In 2016 she won a Billboard Women in Music Award and a Soul Train Award and as nominated for two Grammy Awards.
